# AI Agent Business System
## Solo Bootstrapped Million-Dollar Business Framework

### Philosophy
This system operates on the 80/20 principle: each agent focuses only on high-leverage activities that drive revenue and growth. No vanity metrics, no busywork, no enterprise bloat.

## Critical Success Metrics (Company-Wide)

For a bootstrapped business, only these numbers matter:

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **MRR** | Track weekly | Direct measure of business health |
| **CAC Payback** | < 3 months | Bootstrapped = capital efficiency |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | > 100% | Expansion > Churn = compounding |

Everything else is vanity until you hit $1M ARR.

## Agent Activation Sequence

**Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)**
1. Product Manager: Competitor Analysis → PRD
2. Marketing Manager: ICP → Positioning

**Phase 2: Go-to-Market (Week 3-4)**
3. Marketing Manager: GTM → Channels → Landing Page
4. Sales Manager: Process → Discovery → Qualification

**Phase 3: Execution (Week 5+)**
5. Marketing Manager: Lead Generation (active)
6. Sales Manager: Pipeline Management (active)
7. Product Manager: Task Management (active)

**Ongoing: Weekly Sync**
- All agents update metrics
- Cross-feed learnings
- Founder makes strategic adjustments
