# Marketing Manager Agent: Positioning & Messaging
## Positioning & Messaging Framework

> **Purpose**: Create consistent, compelling messaging that differentiates you from competitors and resonates with your ICP.
> **Update Frequency**: Quarterly or after major product/market shifts
> **Feeds Into**: Landing Page, Channels, Sales Discovery

---

## 1. Positioning Statement

### Core Positioning Formula

```
FOR [Target Customer]
WHO [Statement of Need/Opportunity]
[Product Name] IS A [Product Category]
THAT [Key Benefit]
UNLIKE [Primary Competitor/Alternative]
WE [Key Differentiator]
```

**Example:**
> FOR B2B SaaS founders
> WHO waste 10+ hours weekly on manual outbound prospecting
> LeadGenius IS A AI-powered lead enrichment platform
> THAT automatically finds and qualifies ideal prospects
> UNLIKE traditional data providers like ZoomInfo
> WE focus exclusively on bootstrapped SaaS with 90% accuracy guarantee

## 4. Messaging by Awareness Stage

### Unaware
*They don't know they have a problem*

**Hook:** [Provocative statement or question]
**Message:** [Educate on the problem]

Example:
> "Most B2B founders spend 40% of their time on lead gen that a $99/month tool can automate."

### Problem Aware
*They know the problem but not the solution*

**Hook:** [Name their pain specifically]
**Message:** [Introduce solution category]

Example:
> "Tired of manually researching prospects on LinkedIn? There's a better way."

### Solution Aware
*They know solutions exist, evaluating options*

**Hook:** [Differentiate from alternatives]
**Message:** [Why you're the best choice]

Example:
> "Unlike enterprise tools built for sales teams, we're built for solo founders who need results, not complexity."

### Product Aware
*They know you, need final push*

**Hook:** [Social proof + urgency]
**Message:** [Specific offer + CTA]

Example:
> "Join 500+ bootstrapped founders who've automated their outbound. Start your free trial."

## 8. Social Proof Framework

### Types of Proof (Prioritized)

1. **Specific Results** (Most powerful)
   - "[Customer] increased [metric] by [X%] in [timeframe]"
   
2. **Recognizable Logos**
   - Companies your ICP respects
   
3. **Testimonial Quotes**
   - Specific, outcome-focused quotes
   
4. **Numbers**
   - Users, revenue generated, time saved
   
5. **Trust Badges**
   - Security certs, integrations, press mentions

### Proof Collection System
```
After every customer success:
1. Ask for specific metrics
2. Request testimonial quote
3. Ask for logo permission
4. Offer case study interview
```
