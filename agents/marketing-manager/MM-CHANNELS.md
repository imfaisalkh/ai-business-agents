## 1. LinkedIn Outbound Playbook

### Why LinkedIn for Bootstrapped Founders
- Direct access to decision makers
- Free to start (upgrade later if needed)
- Highly targeted (job title, company size, industry)
- Builds personal brand simultaneously

### Daily LinkedIn Routine (60-90 minutes)

```
Morning Block (30-45 min):
□ Check notifications, respond to comments
□ Send 15-20 connection requests to ICP
□ Comment on 5-10 posts from ICP/influencers

Afternoon Block (30-45 min):
□ Send 10-15 follow-up messages
□ Publish content (if posting day)
□ Engage with new connections
```

### Connection Request Strategy

**Targeting Filters:**
- Job Title: [Your ICP titles]
- Company Size: [Your ICP range]
- Industry: [Your ICP industries]
- Geography: [Your target regions]
- Posted recently: Yes (active users)

**Connection Message Templates:**

*Template 1: Shared Context*
```
Hey [Name], noticed you're [something specific about their profile/content]. 
I help [ICP] with [outcome]. Would love to connect.
```

*Template 2: Mutual Connection*
```
Hi [Name], saw we're both connected with [Mutual]. 
I'm working on [brief description]. Thought it'd be good to connect.
```

*Template 3: Content Appreciation*
```
[Name], your post about [topic] was spot on. 
Especially the part about [specific detail]. Let's connect.
```

**Rules:**
- ❌ No pitching in connection requests
- ❌ No generic "I'd like to add you to my network"
- ✅ Always personalize with something specific
- ✅ Keep under 300 characters

### Follow-Up Messaging Sequence

**Day 1 (After acceptance):**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]! 

Quick question: What's your biggest challenge with [pain point area] right now?

Not pitching anything—just curious what folks like you are dealing with.
```

**Day 3-5 (If no response):**
```
Hey [Name], hope you're having a good week.

I've been helping [similar company/role] with [specific result]. 

Any chance you're dealing with similar challenges?
```

**Day 7-10 (Soft pitch):**
```
[Name], I created [resource/tool] specifically for [their role/company type].

It's helped [similar people] [achieve result].

Worth a quick look? [link] or I can give you a 10-min walkthrough.
```

### LinkedIn Content Strategy

**Post Frequency:** 3-5x/week

**Content Mix:**
| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Personal Story | 1x/week | Build connection, show humanity |
| Educational/How-to | 2x/week | Demonstrate expertise |
| Industry Observation | 1x/week | Thought leadership |
| Product/Results | 1x/week | Social proof, awareness |

**Content Frameworks That Work:**

*Framework 1: Contrarian Take*
```
Everyone says [common belief].
I disagree.
Here's why:
[3-5 points]
[Call to action]
```

*Framework 2: Step-by-Step*
```
How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]:
Step 1: [Action]
Step 2: [Action]
Step 3: [Action]
The key insight: [Lesson]
```

*Framework 3: Before/After*
```
6 months ago: [Painful situation]
Today: [Better situation]
What changed:
[Key changes/actions]
```

**Posting Best Practices:**
- Post between 7-9am or 5-7pm (local time of ICP)
- Use line breaks liberally
- Hook in first line (shows in feed preview)
- End with engagement prompt (question)
- Reply to all comments within 1 hour

## 2. Twitter/X Playbook

### Daily Twitter Routine (30-45 minutes)

```
□ Reply to 10-15 tweets from ICP/influencers (valuable comments, not "great post")
□ Quote tweet 1-2 relevant posts with your take
□ Post 1-2 tweets
□ Engage with replies to your tweets
```

### Content Strategy

**Thread Framework:**
```
Hook tweet: [Provocative statement or promise]

Tweet 2: Context/setup

Tweets 3-7: Main content (one idea per tweet)

Final tweet: Summary + CTA (follow, reply, link)
```

**Solo Tweet Types:**
- Hot take on industry trend
- Quick tip (actionable)
- Lesson learned (story format)
- Question to audience
- Behind-the-scenes

### Growth Tactics
- Reply to big accounts (gets visibility to their audience)
- Use relevant hashtags sparingly (1-2 max)
- Pin your best-performing tweet
- Cross-post top LinkedIn content (reformatted)

## 3. Content/SEO Playbook

### Keyword Strategy

**Finding Keywords:**
1. List problems your ICP searches for
2. Use free tools: Ubersuggest, AnswerThePublic, AlsoAsked
3. Check competitor content for gaps
4. Look at Reddit/Quora questions

**Keyword Prioritization:**
| Keyword | Search Volume | Difficulty | Intent | Priority |
|---------|---------------|------------|--------|----------|
| [keyword] | [volume] | [1-100] | [info/transact] | [H/M/L] |

### Content Production Workflow

**Weekly Content Rhythm:**
```
Monday: Outline new piece based on keyword
Tuesday: Draft first version
Wednesday: Edit and add visuals
Thursday: Publish + promote
Friday: Repurpose for social
```

### On-Page SEO Checklist
- [ ] Target keyword in title (front-loaded)
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Target keyword in one H2
- [ ] Internal links to related content
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Meta description written (155 chars)
- [ ] Images with alt text
- [ ] URL is short and includes keyword

## 4. Cold Email Playbook

### Technical Setup
```
Required:
- Custom domain (not your main domain)
- Warm up email for 2 weeks before sending
- SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- Send limits: 50/day per email address max
```

**Recommended Tools (Bootstrap-friendly):**
- Email finding: Hunter.io, Apollo.io
- Sending: Instantly, Lemlist, Smartlead
- Warm-up: Built into above tools

### Email Sequence Structure

**Email 1: The Hook**
```
Subject: [Specific observation about their company]

[Name],

[Specific personalization - 1-2 sentences about them]

[Problem statement that resonates with their role]

[Brief intro to what you do - 1 sentence]

[Soft CTA: "Worth a conversation?" or "Open to learning more?"]

[Your name]
```

**Email 2 (Day 3): The Value Add**
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

[Name],

Thought this might be useful: [Link to relevant resource, case study, or tool]

We helped [similar company] [specific result].

If you're dealing with [pain point], happy to show you how in 15 min.

[Your name]
```

**Email 3 (Day 7): The Breakup**
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

[Name],

I'll keep this short.

Either:
a) This isn't a priority right now (totally fine)
b) You're not the right person (who should I reach out to?)
c) Timing is just off (when should I circle back?)

Either way, no hard feelings.

[Your name]
```

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate target: >50%
- Reply rate target: >5%
- Meeting book rate: >1%

## 5. Community Marketing Playbook

### Community Selection Criteria
- [ ] ICP is active there
- [ ] Not oversaturated with competitors
- [ ] Allows genuine participation (not just promotion)
- [ ] Active (posts get replies within 24hr)

### Community Engagement Rules

**DO:**
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Share genuine insights and experiences
- Celebrate others' wins
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Share your failures and lessons

**DON'T:**
- Drop links without context
- Pitch in every interaction
- Only show up when you want something
- Ignore community guidelines

### Value-First Framework
```
Weeks 1-4: Only give value. Zero asks.
Weeks 5-8: Mention your work naturally when relevant.
Week 9+: Soft promotion when you've earned trust.
```

## 6. Partnership Marketing Playbook

### Partnership Types

| Type | Description | Effort | Potential |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| Integration Partners | Your product works with theirs | High | High |
| Content Partners | Co-create content, webinars | Medium | Medium |
| Referral Partners | Mutual referrals | Low | Medium |
| Affiliate Partners | They promote for commission | Low | Variable |

### Partnership Outreach Template
```
Subject: Partnership idea: [Specific mutual benefit]

[Name],

I run [Your Company]—we help [ICP] [outcome].

I noticed your customers likely also need [what you offer]. 

Idea: [Specific partnership proposal]

Potential benefit for you: [What they get]
What we'd bring: [Your contribution]

Worth exploring?

[Your name]
```
