## 3. Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Model Selection

| Model | Best For | Bootstrapped Fit |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| Flat Rate | Simple products, predictable value | ✅ Easy to sell |
| Per Seat | Team products, growing orgs | ⚠️ Complex for solo |
| Usage-Based | Variable consumption | ⚠️ Unpredictable revenue |
| Tiered | Feature differentiation | ✅ Good upsell path |

### Your Pricing Structure

```
Tier 1: [Name] - $[X]/month
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Limitation]

Tier 2: [Name] - $[X]/month
- Everything in Tier 1
- [Additional Feature]
- [Higher Limit]

Tier 3: [Name] - $[X]/month
- Everything in Tier 2
- [Premium Feature]
- [Unlimited/Highest Limit]
```

### Pricing Psychology

- [ ] Anchor with highest tier first
- [ ] Highlight "most popular" tier
- [ ] Use .99 or round numbers consistently
- [ ] Annual discount: [X]% (recommend 15-20%)
- [ ] Money-back guarantee: [X] days

### Price Validation Checklist

- [ ] Compared to competitor pricing
- [ ] Tested with 5+ ICP prospects
- [ ] Calculated unit economics (LTV > 3x CAC)
- [ ] Considered bootstrapped budget constraints

## 6. First 100 Customers Playbook

### Phase 1: Friends & Network (Customers 1-10)
```
Timeline: Week 1-4
Channels: Direct outreach, warm intros
Target: 10 paying customers
Actions:
- [ ] List 50 people in network who might buy/refer
- [ ] Personal outreach to each
- [ ] Offer "founding member" pricing
- [ ] Ask for referrals from each conversion
```

### Phase 2: Outbound & Community (Customers 11-30)
```
Timeline: Week 5-12
Channels: LinkedIn outbound, community participation
Target: 20 additional paying customers
Actions:
- [ ] Daily LinkedIn outreach (10-20 personalized messages)
- [ ] Join 3-5 communities where ICP hangs out
- [ ] Share value before pitching
- [ ] Book discovery calls from interested leads
```

### Phase 3: Content & Scale (Customers 31-100)
```
Timeline: Week 13-26
Channels: Content marketing, referrals, partnerships
Target: 70 additional paying customers
Actions:
- [ ] Publish 2-3x/week on primary platform
- [ ] Build referral program
- [ ] Launch on Product Hunt / similar
- [ ] Explore partnership opportunities
```

## 7. Competitive Response Plan

### Competitor Monitoring
```
Track monthly:
- [ ] Competitor pricing changes
- [ ] New feature launches
- [ ] Their content/positioning shifts
- [ ] Customer reviews (G2, Capterra)
```

### Response Playbook

| Competitor Move | Your Response |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Price cut | Emphasize value, not price |
| New feature (you have) | Create comparison content |
| New feature (you don't) | Assess demand, prioritize roadmap |
| Aggressive marketing | Double down on differentiation |

## 9. Risk Mitigation

### GTM Risks & Contingencies

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Primary channel saturates | Medium | High | Develop secondary channel now |
| Competitor price war | Low | Medium | Focus on differentiation, not price |
| Slow initial traction | High | High | Extend runway, iterate on messaging |
| Product-market fit issues | Medium | High | Customer feedback loops |
