# Revenue Model & Projections

> **Purpose:** Financial model for TeamPulse. Projects revenue based on pricing strategy and growth assumptions.
>
> **Fits in:** Uses pricing from product/06-pricing-strategy.md. Feeds into Unit Economics (02) and Burn/Runway (03).

## Revenue Model Summary

**Business model:** B2B SaaS subscription
**Pricing model:** Per-employee-per-month (PEPM)
**Price point:** $6-8 PEPM (annual), $10 PEPM (monthly)
**Target ACV:** $1,200-1,800 (10-15 employee teams)
**Revenue goal (6 months):** $3-5K MRR

---

## Growth Assumptions

### Customer Acquisition

| Phase | Timeline | New Customers/Month | Method |
|-------|----------|---------------------|--------|
| Beta | Month 1-2 | 5-10 (free) | LinkedIn outreach |
| Early | Month 3-4 | 3-5 (paid) | LinkedIn + content |
| Growth | Month 5-6 | 5-8 (paid) | Scaling what works |

### Conversion Rates (Assumptions)

| Stage | Conversion | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Trial signup | 10% of qualified leads | From discovery calls |
| Trial to paid | 25-35% | Industry benchmark: 15-25% |
| Annual vs monthly | 70% annual | With discount incentive |

### Churn Assumptions

| Metric | Assumption | Rationale |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| Monthly logo churn | 3-5% | SMB typical, reviews are sticky |
| Net revenue retention | 100-110% | Expansion from team growth |
| Gross churn | 5-8% | Some SMBs will close/churn |

## Revenue Drivers

### Primary Drivers

| Driver | Impact | How to Improve |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| New customer acquisition | High | More outreach, better conversion |
| Average team size | Medium | Target larger teams (20-50) |
| Annual vs monthly mix | Medium | Stronger annual incentives |
| Trial conversion | High | Better onboarding, demo quality |

### Secondary Drivers

| Driver | Impact | How to Improve |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Expansion (team growth) | Medium | Customer success, usage tracking |
| Pricing increase | Low | Market validation, value adds |
| Churn reduction | Medium | Better onboarding, support |

## Revenue Mix

### By Billing Period (Target)

| Billing | % of Customers | % of Revenue | Rationale |
|---------|----------------|--------------|-----------|
| Annual | 70% | 60% | Lower price, but guaranteed |
| Monthly | 30% | 40% | Higher price, more flexible |

### By Customer Size (Target)

| Team Size | % of Customers | % of Revenue | Rationale |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|-----------|
| 10-15 | 40% | 30% | Easier to close, lower ACV |
| 15-25 | 40% | 45% | Sweet spot |
| 25-50 | 20% | 25% | Harder to close, higher ACV |

## Seasonality

### Review Cycle Timing

| Quarter | Buying Activity | Notes |
|---------|-----------------|-------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | High | Q4 reviews trigger buying |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Medium | Mid-year reviews |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | Low | Summer slowdown |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | High | Annual review prep |

**Implication:** Plan launches and campaigns around Q1 and Q4
