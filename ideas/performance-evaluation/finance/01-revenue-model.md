# Revenue Model & Pricing

## Business Model

**SaaS Subscription (Per-Employee-Per-Month)**

- Monthly or annual billing
- All employees who receive evaluations are counted
- 14-day free trial (no credit card required)
- Self-service checkout (Stripe)

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter: $6 PEPM (Monthly) | $61/employee/year (Annual - 15% off)
- **Target:** 5-25 employees
- **All features:** Self-review + gap analysis, peer feedback, templates, goals, analytics
- **Support:** Email support

### Growth: $7 PEPM (Monthly) | $72/employee/year (Annual - 15% off)
- **Target:** 26-100 employees
- **Everything in Starter, plus:** Custom templates, HRIS integrations, priority support

### Enterprise: Custom Pricing ($6-8 PEPM based on volume)
- **Target:** 100+ employees
- **Everything in Growth, plus:** SSO, API access, dedicated CSM, SLA, SOC 2 docs

## Revenue Drivers

1. **New customer acquisition** (70% of growth)
2. **Team expansion** (20% of growth - customers add employees)
3. **Upsells** (10% of growth - Starter → Growth tier)

**Target Net Revenue Retention:** 105-110% (team growth offsets churn)

---

## Payment Processing

**Stripe:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

**Monthly cost example:**
- Customer pays $120/month → Stripe fee $3.78 → Net revenue $116.22

**Annual cost example:**
- Customer pays $1,020/year → Stripe fee $29.88 → Net revenue $990.12
