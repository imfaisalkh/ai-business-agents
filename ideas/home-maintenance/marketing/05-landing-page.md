## Copy Guidelines

### Headlines
- Lead with the problem or outcome, not the product
- Use specific numbers when possible
- Avoid jargon ("field service management platform")

**Good:**
- "Stop losing jobs to chaos"
- "Get paid 10 days faster"
- "Save 70% compared to Jobber"

**Bad:**
- "The future of field service management"
- "Comprehensive workforce optimization"
- "Best-in-class scheduling solution"

### Body Copy
- Short sentences. Active voice.
- Use "you" and "your" (not "businesses" or "users")
- Include specific benefits, not features
- Address objections before they arise

### CTAs
- Primary: "Start Free Trial" (not "Get Started")
- Secondary: "Schedule Demo" (for those who want human contact)
- Tertiary: "Watch 3-min Demo" (for those not ready to commit)

---

## Visual Guidelines

### Screenshots
- Use real product screenshots
- Show filled-in data (not empty states)
- Highlight the UI element being discussed
- Mobile screenshots for mobile features

### Colors
- Primary: Trust-building blue or green
- Accent: Orange or yellow for CTAs
- Neutral: Gray for text and backgrounds
- Avoid: Red (associated with errors/alerts)

### Typography
- Large headline font (48-64px)
- Readable body text (18-20px)
- High contrast (dark text on light background)
- Sans-serif for clean, modern feel

---

## Conversion Optimization

### Trust Signals
- Customer testimonials with real names and companies
- "Trusted by X home service businesses"
- Security badges (SSL, payment security)
- "No credit card required" prominently displayed

### Objection Handling
| Objection | Where addressed |
|-----------|-----------------|
| "I'm not tech-savvy" | FAQ: "Set up takes 15 minutes" |
| "What if it doesn't work for me?" | "14-day free trial, cancel anytime" |
| "Why is it so cheap?" | Comparison table explains value |
| "Is my data safe?" | Footer: Security/privacy link |

### Friction Reduction
- No credit card for trial
- Single email field to start (not full form)
- "Continue with Google" option
- Progressive profiling (ask for details later)

## Mobile Considerations

- **Hero:** Stack vertically, larger CTA button
- **Screenshots:** Single column, swipeable carousel
- **Testimonials:** One at a time, swipeable
- **FAQ:** Collapsed by default (accordion)
- **CTA:** Sticky button at bottom of screen

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- Page load under 3 seconds
- Optimize images (WebP format)
- Lazy load below-fold content
- Minimal JavaScript

### SEO
- Title: "[Product Name] - Field Service Software for Small Businesses"
- Meta description: "Scheduling, invoicing, and worker management for home service businesses. $79/month flat. No per-user fees. Start free trial."
- H1: Main headline
- Alt text for all images

### Analytics
- Track: Page views, scroll depth, CTA clicks, form starts, form completions
- Set up conversion funnel in analytics
- Heatmaps for first 1000 visitors (Hotjar free tier)
