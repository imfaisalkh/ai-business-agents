## North Star Metric

**Close Rate: Trial-to-Paid Conversion**

Definition: Percentage of trial signups that convert to paying customers.

**Formula:** (Paid customers / Trial signups) × 100

**Targets:**
| Phase | Target | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Validation (W1-4) | 25% | Learning, expect lower |
| Traction (M2-3) | 30% | Process maturing |
| Growth (M4-6) | 35%+ | Optimized funnel |

**Why this metric?**
- Directly tied to revenue
- Reflects quality of leads AND sales process
- Actionable - can improve with better qualification, onboarding, demos

## Funnel Metrics

### Full Funnel Tracking

```
Outreach → Response → Qualified → Discovery → Demo → Trial → Activated → Paid
```

| Stage | Target Conversion | Cumulative (per 1000 outreach) |
|-------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| Outreach → Response | 5% | 50 responses |
| Response → Qualified | 70% | 35 qualified |
| Qualified → Discovery | 50% | 17 discovery calls |
| Discovery → Demo | 80% | 14 demos |
| Demo → Trial | 60% | 8 trials |
| Trial → Activated | 70% | 6 activated |
| Activated → Paid | 50% | 3 customers |

**Overall conversion:** 0.3% (3 customers per 1000 outreach)

### Funnel Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Tracked By |
|-------|------------|------------|
| **Outreach** | Cold email, LinkedIn, contact form sent | Sent count |
| **Response** | Any reply (positive or negative) | Reply received |
| **Qualified** | Meets BANT+ criteria | Scorecard 18+ |
| **Discovery** | Completed discovery call | Call logged |
| **Demo** | Saw product demo | Demo completed |
| **Trial** | Created account | Account created |
| **Activated** | Completed first job + invoice | Event triggered |
| **Paid** | Subscription started | Stripe webhook |

## Tracking Tools

### Phase 1: Spreadsheet (0-50 customers)

**Google Sheets tabs:**
1. **Pipeline** - All active deals with stages
2. **Activity Log** - Daily outreach and call tracking
3. **Conversions** - Won/lost deals with reasons
4. **Metrics** - Calculated rates and trends

### Phase 2: CRM (50+ customers)

**Recommended:** HubSpot Free
- Contact and deal management
- Email tracking
- Basic reporting
- Free for small teams

### Key Reports to Build

1. **Conversion funnel** - Stage-to-stage conversion
2. **Source performance** - Conversion by lead source
3. **Activity vs results** - Are more calls = more customers?
4. **Time analysis** - Days in each stage
5. **Loss reasons** - Why deals are lost
